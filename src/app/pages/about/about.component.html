What is Highlight?

Highlight is a live music discovery platform built for Ireland's busking community. Buskers post their gigs — fans find them on the map, follow their favourite performers, and never miss a set happening just around the corner.

●

Live Map

See every active and upcoming gig plotted on a real-time map. Filter by type, distance, and time.

●

Follow Buskers

Follow the performers you love. Their events appear on your personal page automatically.

●

Post Your Gig

Buskers create an account, pin their location on the map, and go live in seconds.

●

Built for Ireland

From Dublin's Grafton Street to Galway's Shop Street — Highlight covers the island.

Built with

Angular Spring Boot Kotlin PostgreSQL JWT Auth Leaflet OpenStreetMap

Full-stack solo project. Spring Boot BFF with JWT cookie auth, BCrypt password hashing, role-based access control, email verification, and a real-time map powered by Leaflet and OpenStreetMap.
